Welcome to iTaK Shield

iTaK Shield protects your privacy when using AI tools. It sits on your computer and automatically removes personal information from your messages before they reach cloud AI services. The AI still understands your request, but never sees your real data.

  • 🛡️ Emails, SSNs, API keys, file paths, and phone numbers are replaced with safe placeholders
  • 🔒 Your data never leaves your machine. Only anonymized text goes to the cloud.
  • ⚡ Works with 25+ AI providers: OpenAI, Anthropic, Gemini, DeepSeek, Mistral, xAI, OpenRouter, Ollama, and more
  • 🧠 The AI still understands context. It knows [EMAIL_1] is an email. It just never sees which email.
  • 🤖 Compatible with agent frameworks like OpenClaw and Agent Zero out of the box
